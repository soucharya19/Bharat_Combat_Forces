About Us

Bharat Combat Forces aims to help people to recover crisis and connect to disaster response systems and get secured.

Bharat Combat Force tracks down the location of the person asking for help during any type of natural disaster and sends help as soon as possible.
Donations made at the Frontline force are directed to an NGO which inturn provides the money to the families affected.
